Harden Trade

The James Harden trade was one of the biggest trades in the NBA over the past few years. The 76ers were finally rid of Ben Simmons and brought in a playmaker and scorer to replace him. While Harden was not fully healthy, he quickly adjusted to his new team and meshed well with his new teammates. He brought a new energy and a hope for the rest of the season and a few years to come. He could actually shoot and is one of the most prolific three-point shooters of all time. A needed change for a 76ers franchise that can't seem to become true contenders.

2022 Playoffs

The Philadelphia 76ers began the 2022 playoffs with title hopes. Soon, like every year, they quickly vanished. Joel Embiid suffered a face injury, Maxey struggled in his first real playoff role, and Harden was clearly still suffering from a previous hamstring injury. The team defeated the bad Toronto Raptors in 6 games. They then fell to the Miami Heat in 6 games in the second round.
